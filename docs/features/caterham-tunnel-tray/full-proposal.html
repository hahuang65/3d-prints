Lid question

Three ways to do the lid. Which appeals?

  • Dovetail slide — the lid has dovetail rails on the sides that slide into matching grooves in the tray walls. No hardware, no rattles, lid can't fly off. Slides rearward to remove. You need ~50 mm of clearance behind the tray to slide it off.
  • Friction fit — the lid sits on a ledge inside the tray with a slight interference fit, or uses small locating pegs. Simplest to print but might bounce loose on rough roads.
  • Hinge + magnetic catch — a printed hinge at the front (near the firewall), with a small magnet or detent at the rear to keep it closed. More parts but feels premium.
